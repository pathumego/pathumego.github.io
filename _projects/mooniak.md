Mooniak is a type foundry and typographic design studio based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, co-founded in 2015. It is the leading studio for multilingual fonts, typography and text technology in Sri Lanka and the region.

## What Mooniak Does

Mooniak pioneers Sinhala and Tamil multi-script fonts for the web and print, and maintains a large collection of Free and Open Source font projects. Beyond typeface design, Mooniak is active in the publishing industry and provides consultancy on:

- Font licensing and type selection
- Typographic design for trilingual (Sinhala, Tamil, English) communications
- Language technology and script encoding
- Multilingual branding and visual identity

## Key Projects

Over the years, Mooniak has developed and published numerous typefaces covering Sinhala, Tamil, Latin, and other scripts. Several of these are distributed under open-source licenses and used widely across Sri Lanka and the South Asian diaspora.

## Role

As founder and Managing Director, the work spans typeface design, font engineering, project management, client relationships, and advocacy for open-source typography in Sri Lanka.
