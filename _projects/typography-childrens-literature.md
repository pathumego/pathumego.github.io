A workshop organised by IBBY Sri Lanka (The International Board on Books for Young People) on 26 January 2019 at Sri Lanka Print Media Academy.

Contributed as a resource person alongside Dr. Sumanthri Samarawickrama and Rashandi Vihara Porambage, representing Akuru Collective and the Department of Integrated Design, University of Moratuwa. The workshop raised awareness on how to design children's story books and the use of Sinhala typography, with 25 enthusiast graphic designers, writers, and illustrators participating.
