Sati Compass is a small accountability group specifically designed to support the flourishing of creative individuals by fostering well-being and resilience. Deriving its name from the Buddhist concept of සති "sati," which signifies mindfulness and awareness, the community also embodies the principles of Self-Actualization and Transformation Intensive (SATI). This unique blend of spiritual grounding, mind-body practice and measurable personal development and accountability techniques aims to cultivate a supportive environment where members can engage deeply with their creative processes while also focusing on personal growth.
